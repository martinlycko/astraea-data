INVESTMENT DATA ANALYST SPECIALIST
LONDON - IN OFFICE REQUIREMENT
COMPETITIVE SALARY & BENEFITS

Are you passionate about turning raw data into valuable insights? Do you have experience in the financial industry and strong technical skills? We are seeking a talented Data Analyst to join our growing team and help us enhance our data capabilities.

THE COMPANY:
They are an investment management firm based in the UK, authorised and regulated by the FCA. The firm is also registered with several financial authorities in the US and is a member of the National Futures Association (NFA). Their team operates globally, with offices globally and over 200 employees.

The firm is committed to delivering consistent long-term returns for their investors, working within a well-defined risk framework. They focus on various investment strategies in both developed and emerging markets.

THE ROLE:
They are looking for an experienced Data Analyst to complement their team as they enhance their data practices, policies, and platforms. You will work closely with the front office, technology teams, and key data consumers, acting as a content and subject-matter expert. Your role will involve significant exposure to various data types used in alpha-generation, with ample opportunities to master different tools and skills.

YOUR SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

Experience in a similar role within the financial industry.
Proven ability to manage and transform raw datasets into actionable insights.
Detailed knowledge of different asset classes.
Familiarity with data vendors and broader industry data.
Experience interrogating vendor data endpoints using Python or SQL.
HOW TO APPLY:
